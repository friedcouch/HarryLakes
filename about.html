About me

Hello, I'm a freelance photographer from Australia. I started doing photography because I enjoyed capturing life's moments and looking back at them later on. I like to say that photographs are sort of like a time machine; you look at them and recall vividly the events that happened even if whatever in the photograph is no more.

It's been half a decade since I picked up my first camera, and I've learnt lots along the way. I'm keen to help you in your next project, big or small. Drop me an email, let's talk!
